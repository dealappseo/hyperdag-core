Contributor responsibilities
By contributing to this repository, you affirm:
- Contributions do not infringe on third-party patents
- You agree to assign patent-relevant claims arising from your contribution to the HyperDAG patent family (per CLA, when finalized)
- You will disclose any prior art you are aware of that is relevant to the contribution
